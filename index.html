Minwoo Kim, Ph.D.

I'm a Staff Engineer at Samsung Research in the Robot Intelligence Team. My core expertise is rooted in vision-based navigation, reinforcement learning, and inverse reinforcement learning for autonomous mobile robots and UAVs. Currently, I am actively engaged in pioneering research on Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for real robot manipulation.
