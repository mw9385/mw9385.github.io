Stable Online Adaptation of Diffusion Policies via Latent-Regularized Steering and Periodic Resetting Under Preparation
Minwoo Kim, Taehan Kim, Wonhyuk Choi, Jihwan Kim
Samsung Research
We propose LRDS, an online RL framework for stable fine-tuning of pretrained diffusion policies in long-horizon, high-precision manipulation. LRDS introduces Gaussian-space regularization with DCT-based noise parameterization, a periodic resetting mechanism, and a distributional critic for bimodal value landscapes. Evaluated on a 44-DoF real-world robot, LRDS achieves 90% task success from 40% within one hour of training.
